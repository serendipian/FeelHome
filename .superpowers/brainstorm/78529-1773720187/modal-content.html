5

Skills & Tools — Tag clouds

Skills tags (Negotiation, CRM...) + Tools tags (WhatsApp, Canva...)

6

KPIs — Targets & Metrics

Revenue targets, conversion rates, response times... with target values

7

Brand Affiliation — Which brands they serve

Feel Home, M Invest, Expats.ma — with brand avatars/colors

My recommendation: For an investor, include all 7 sections but organized as a scrollable panel. The header + responsibilities give the "what does this person do" answer in 2 seconds. KPIs show accountability. Compensation shows cost structure. Skills/tools show professionalism. Keep it all read-only — no editing in this view.
