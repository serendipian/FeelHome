Which layout best communicates hierarchy & relationships to an investor?

A — Orbital / Solar System

Director at center, concentric rings by tier. Animated pulses show command flow. Very visual, unique, strong "wow" factor.

Classic org chart, modernized. Top-down hierarchy with animated particles flowing through command chain. Universally understood.

Instant clarity Professional Familiar

C — Constellation Network

Organic network graph with glowing nodes and animated particle flow. Feels like a living ecosystem. Very tech-forward & modern.

Most wow Tech-forward Less obvious hierarchy

My Recommendation

Option A (Orbital) is my top pick for investor pitches. It creates an immediate visual metaphor — the Director as the "sun" with tiers orbiting around. It's unique (investors rarely see this), the hierarchy is crystal clear from center→outward, and animated pulses create a living, breathing feel. Combined with click-to-expand lateral modals, it's both stunning and functional.

Option B is the safest choice (everyone understands org charts). Option C is the most "artistic" but hierarchy is harder to read at a glance.
